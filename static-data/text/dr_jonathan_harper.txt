Dr. Jonathan Harper is a distinguished professor of astrophysics at MIT. He specializes in black hole physics and gravitational wave detection. With over 20 years of experience, he has led numerous research projects and published over 80 papers in prestigious journals. His groundbreaking work on LIGO contributed to the first detection of gravitational waves in 2015. He holds a PhD from Caltech and has received multiple awards including the Breakthrough Prize in Fundamental Physics.

Dr. Harper is also passionate about science education and has mentored dozens of graduate students. He frequently speaks at international conferences and has appeared in several documentaries about space and physics. His current research focuses on the detection of primordial gravitational waves that could reveal secrets about the early universe.
